About Our School

Welcome to Justine-Thomas

To provide quality education in Enugu, Nigeria, Paul and Justina Amoge Iloabachie founded Justine-Thomas Primary School in 1989. The school's name is a tribute to both Justina, who passed away just as the project began, and Paul's father, Thomas, combining their first names in her honor.

The school eventually took off in September 1989 with just 12 pupils and 2 teachers. Today, Justine-Thomas Primary School has grown to become one of the leading primary schools in Enugu, with a student population of over 100 pupils and a dedicated team of qualified teachers. As at today, the school has produced over 5,000 alumni who have gone on to excel in various fields owing to our:

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Child-centered learning approach
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Modern teaching facilities
  • Extracurricular activities
